Amazon search

Description:

For our project of cordova I have created an Android app.

In the app you can query different amazon stores, and you automatically get the top results back for each selected country you searched for.

App info:

Name: Amazon search
creator: Grevendonk Tim
project: Cordova 2ITF 2021
platform: Android & browsers

Features:

Select the countries you want to get Amazon items from.
Give a keyword as the search item, and results wil populate below.

History is kept on the third tab.

items can be saved and found back in the fourth tab.

Technicalities:

  • History and saved lists can be fully deleted.
  • Individual saved items can be deleted.
  • If no text is entered, the app will not search for items.
  • Items are still present after fully closing the app.
